Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to allow for people online to become aware of my business, get in contact with us about our facilities, learn about our facilities and what sort of services we offer. They can see what types of rafting experiences we provide ranging from novice to rafting for more experienced individuals.

Audience

The target audience ranges from those mildly interested in rafting to those who raft as their main hobby. We will offer entry level information and experiences for those newer to rafting as well as more intermediate to advanced experiences for those who are more aquainted with the hobby.
